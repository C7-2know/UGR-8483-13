Mekedonia's Community

The peoples helped by this association

many peoples are helped by mekedonia since it starts. It holds elders from street, person's with disability and person's with mental illness. many peoples in the association has served their country in their profession for a long time. many of disabled persons were Solders; Doctors, engineers, teachers, captians are also found their. the association provide health treatment for its beneficiaries. by taking the treatment many persons get thier helth back and some of them could able to join their community. many of them be there in mekedonia and help the association in their capability.

Contribution Of beneficiaries

once the beneficiaries get their health back, they start to help the association by participating in lot of fields. There is an art room in the compound and some beneficiaries contribute there by drawing pictures. also there is a room where many hand crafts made, lot of beneficiaries work there. some beneficiaries participate on taking care of patients, some on preparing meal for the beneficiaries, some on other labour intensive activities when the association need. some sew cloths when the other beneficiaries cloth pored or make the aided cloths fitted for the beneficiaries.

How they interact with each other?

the peoples in mekedonia have social life in the compound. they have worship program when they pray to their God, based on their religion. the beneficiaries also use a library if they want to read. there is also room to play a game each other and they play. there is also sport room where the beneficiaries do physical exercise. the beneficiaries also get there bed in the living room according to their health status and the healthier peoples get the chance to talk to each other.

Other workers in the Association

there are more than paid workers and variable number of volunter workers in mekedonia. their fields are preparing a meal, cleaning the rooms, taking care of patients, cleaning the garden...
