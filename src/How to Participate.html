What activities You will do?

do you want to be part of this volunteer service? the only thing required from you is your willingness to help others. the main thing the mekedonya beneficiaries want is a person that could spend time with them, play with them and a person that will chat with them. you can be part of the association by visiting the beneficiaries, by providing food and cloth, by providing monetary aid As there are many peoples in the Association there are many activities you may do if you can come physically.

  • baking injera
  • prepare a dish
  • talking with them
  • singing with them
  • make the members hair
  • make their nail
  • washing dish
  • draw pictures

Programs you can have with us

You can celebrate your special days with us. wedding ceremonies, Birth day party, graduation ceremony, memorial day, sedeka and any other programs can held in our auditorium. the decor and design will be held as you want.
